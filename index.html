TL;DR Cloud (your personal journalist in the cloud)

Imagine the future where you will have a personal news reporter working just for you. Having an own news reporter opens almost endless opportunities around news consumption. Just a simple list of thing this virtual news reporter can do for you:

  • provide you TL;DR that you can read in 2 minutes of most essential facts that happen during the last day, collected from your favorite news channels
  • when you need, it can summarize news article for you, so you do not have to open the site and read full page
  • it can adapt to your preferences by chatting with you around the quality of information.
